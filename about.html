About Internee.pk

Connecting students with meaningful internship opportunities across Pakistan

Our Story

Bridging the gap between education and industry

Founded in 2020, Internee.pk was born out of a simple observation: too many talented students were graduating without practical experience, while companies struggled to find qualified entry-level talent.

Our mission is to create a platform that connects students with meaningful internship opportunities, helping them gain real-world experience while providing companies with access to Pakistan's brightest young minds.
